## üß† Overview

### E-commerce Platform is a comprehensive e-commerce solution designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for users. This platform allows users to browse products, add items to their cart, and complete purchases securely. The project includes features such as product listings, a shopping cart, and a checkout process, all integrated with a robust backend to manage inventory and orders.

## ‚ú® Core Capabilities

- **Product Listings**: Users can view a wide range of products with detailed descriptions and images.
- **Shopping Cart**: Users can add items to their cart, view the total price, and remove items as needed.
- **Checkout Process**: A secure checkout process that includes payment options and order confirmation.
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and registration for users to manage their accounts.
- **Responsive Design**: The platform is designed to work seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.
