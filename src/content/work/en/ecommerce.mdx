## 🧠 Overview

E-commerce Platform is a comprehensive solution designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for users. This platform allows users to browse products, add items to their cart, and complete purchases securely. The project includes features such as product listings, a shopping cart, and a checkout process, all integrated with a robust backend to manage inventory and orders.
